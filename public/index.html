📖 How to Shop on Mellowluv

Follow these steps for a smooth purchase 💕

1
Browse & Select — Pick your favourite thrift pieces or handmade jewellery.
🚫
Don't rush — check size, description and price carefully before adding to cart.
2
Add to Cart — Click "Add to Cart" or pre-book upcoming drops.
🚫
Don't add duplicate items — check your cart before checkout.
3
Checkout — Fill in your name, phone, Instagram ID and delivery address correctly.
🚫
Don't enter wrong Instagram ID — we may need to contact you about shipping charges.
4
Pay via UPI — Scan the QR code or pay to the UPI ID shown. Include your order number in the note.
🚫
Don't pay to a different UPI ID or send incomplete amount — it will delay verification.
5
Upload Screenshot — On the order success page, upload your payment screenshot. We will verify it shortly.
🚫
Don't upload a fake or edited screenshot — your order will be rejected.
6
Wait for Verification — Once verified, we will ship your order. You'll get a notification.
🚫
Don't message repeatedly for status — check tracking page or wait for our confirmation.
7
Track Your Order — Use the Track page with your order number to see real-time updates.
🚫
Don't forget to save your order ID — you'll need it for tracking and support.
