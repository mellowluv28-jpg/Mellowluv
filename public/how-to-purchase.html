📖 How to Shop on Mellowluv

Follow these simple steps for a smooth and smart purchase 💕

1
Browse & Select — Explore our Thrift Store for curated pre-loved pieces or Mellowluv for handmade jewellery. Click on any item to see full details.
🚫
Don't rush — check the size, description, material, and price carefully before adding to cart.
2
Add to Cart — Click "Add to Cart" on any product. You can also pre-book upcoming drops from the homepage banner or Pre-Book page.
🚫
Don't add duplicate items — review your cart before checkout.
3
Checkout — Fill in your name, phone number, Instagram ID, and full delivery address correctly. Double check everything.
🚫
Don't enter a wrong Instagram ID — we may need to reach you if shipping charges are higher based on your order quantity.
4
Pay via UPI — After placing the order, you will see a QR code and UPI ID. Make the payment and include your order number in the payment note.
🚫
Don't pay to a different UPI ID or send an incomplete amount — this will delay verification.
5
Upload Screenshot — On the order success page, upload your payment screenshot. We will verify your payment and confirm the order.
🚫
Don't upload a fake or edited screenshot — your order will be rejected and reverted to unpaid.
6
Wait for Verification — Once verified, we will process and ship your order. You will receive updates.
🚫
Don't message repeatedly asking for status updates — check the tracking page or wait for our confirmation.
7
Track Your Order — Go to the Track page and enter your order ID. You can see real-time status updates.
🚫
Don't forget to save your order ID — you need it for tracking and any support requests.
8
Custom Orders — Want something unique? DM us on Instagram with your idea and we'll create it for you!
🚫
Don't place a custom order through the regular checkout — always DM us first so we can discuss pricing and details.
