📜 Store Policies

Please read carefully before placing an order 💕

⚠️

By placing an order, you agree to all policies below.

If you have any questions, DM us on Instagram @mellowluvv_ before ordering.

❌

NO COD — Full Prepayment Required

All orders must be paid in advance via UPI. Orders are confirmed only after payment is verified.

⏱️

Processing Time: 10–15 Days

Handmade jewellery takes time and care. Please allow 10–15 days for making + dispatch. We do not rush orders unless you opt for urgent delivery.

⚡

Urgent Order: ₹50 Extra

Need it fast? Select "Urgent" at checkout for ₹50 extra. We'll make it in 2–3 days and ship it out. This covers priority making and faster dispatch.

🚫

No Cancellations / Refunds / Returns

We do not accept cancellations, refunds, or returns unless the mistake is from our side (wrong product, wrong size shipped, etc.). Please choose carefully before ordering.

💬

Please Be Patient

I have a life outside this business too. Please don't complain about dispatch time — each piece is handmade with love and takes its own time. If you want it early, place an urgent order.

📦

After Dispatch: Courier Responsibility

Once the package is handed over to the courier, we are not responsible for delays, lost packages, or damage during transit. The courier company handles all delivery-related issues after dispatch.

🎥

Unboxing Video Required for Damage Claims

If your package arrives damaged, you must have a clear unboxing video showing the damage. This is the only proof we accept. Without it, we cannot process a claim with the courier.

✅

Order Confirmation

Your order is confirmed only after we verify your payment. Until then, the order stays in "unpaid" status. We will notify you once it's confirmed.

🛡️

Mistake From Our Side

If we send the wrong product, wrong size, or a defective item, we will replace it or issue a refund. Please reach out to us on Instagram within 48 hours of delivery with your unboxing video.
